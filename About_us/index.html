The Story Behind This Website

After completing my B.Tech in Civil Engineering, I joined the design and proof-checking department of a PSU under Indian Railways. My job involved reviewing structural design drawings, verifying calculations and checking compliance with Indian Standard codes on a daily basis.

The work was demanding and detail-oriented. Every single calculation had to be cross-checked against IS 456, IS 13920 and other codes — manually, every time, for every project. I found myself repeatedly going through the same clauses, recalculating the same intermediate steps and verifying the same code checks from scratch.

"There was no quick, reliable tool to cross-check a beam design on the fly — something that would show you the intermediate steps, flag code violations and give you confidence in the numbers within seconds."

That gap is what Construction-Calculator was built to fill. It is not a design automation tool — it is a proof-checking aid. It helps engineers quickly verify whether a given section and reinforcement satisfies IS code requirements, see all intermediate values transparently and catch mistakes before they go further.

Whether you are a student checking your assignment, a site engineer doing a quick sanity check or a proof-checker reviewing a submission — this tool is built for you.

What This Tool Is — and Is Not

✅ What it IS

A quick proof-checking and verification tool. Enter your section and reinforcement details, and instantly see whether the design satisfies IS code requirements — with full intermediate values and clause references.

❌ What it is NOT

This is not a certified design software. Results should always be verified by a qualified structural engineer. Do not use outputs directly for construction without professional review and stamping.

📋 Transparent Calculations

Every intermediate value is shown — Ast, τv, τc, k factor, development length, and more. No black box. You see exactly what formula was used and which IS clause it came from.

🆓 Always Free

No subscription, no login, no paywall. Built as a free resource for engineering students and professionals across India. Supported by advertisements.

Indian Standards Referenced

Calculations are based on the following Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) codes. All clause references are shown alongside results:

IS 456 : 2000 IS 13920 : 2018 IS 1893 : 2016 IS 1893 : 2025 SP 16 : 1980 IS 875
